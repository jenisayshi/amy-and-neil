Q & A

Before The Day

How do I RSVP?
Please let us know if you can join us by filling out the form on the RSVP section of our wedding website. We kindly ask that everyone RSVP by Friday 7th March 2025 so we can finalise numbers within the venue.

The Day

What time should I arrive?
We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the ceremony begins to allow time for seating and to ensure a prompt start.

What should I wear?
We recommend typical wedding attire to celebrate the occasion, but your comfort is what's most important to us. Please feel free to wear what you like; our suggestion is just a recommendation :)

Can I take photos during the ceremony?
We kindly request an 'unplugged' ceremony - please can all guests turn off their devices and refrain from taking photos during the ceremony. Our professional photographer will capture all the special moments, and we will happily share the photos with you afterward!!

Can I bring a plus one?
For our day ceremony and reception, we have a limited guest list due to venue capacity. However, if you'd like to bring a plus one, they are more than welcome to join us for the evening celebrations - evening guests are also welcome to bring a plus one. Please let us know if you'll be bringing someone by indicating it on the RSVP form.

Extras

Can I give you a Wedding Gift?
Your presence at our wedding is the most meaningful gift we could ask for - we are truly grateful to have you share in our special day! :) However, if you still wish to honour us with a gift, a contribution towards our honeymoon would be greatly appreciated :) Since we already have everything we need for our new home, your generosity will help us create unforgettable memories as we begin this new chapter together.
